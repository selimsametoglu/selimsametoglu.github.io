Privacy Policy

Effective date: 07/07/2025

Thank you for visiting my personal website. I value your privacy and want to be transparent about the information this site collects and how it is used.

1. Information Collected

This website does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

However, it uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google, to help understand how visitors interact with the site.

Google Analytics may collect:

  • General location information (e.g., country)
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring websites or search engines

This data is aggregated and anonymized. I use it to understand overall traffic trends and improve the user experience on my site.

2. Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to track user behavior on the site. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer not to be tracked in this way.

3. Data Sharing

I do not share any data collected from this site with third parties, except as needed to operate Google Analytics.

4. Your Choices

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using Google's browser add-on.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
